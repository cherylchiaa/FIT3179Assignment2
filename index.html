Petroleum Sales by State (2020-2024)

Why NSW has the highest sales?

NSW has highest population among other states in Australia which means greater demand of petroleum for transportation.

Why Northern Territory has lowest sales?

Tasmania is the least populated state, but Northern Territory has lower sales. This could be due to its urbanisation, tourism, and reliance on transport logistics.

Refinery Production (2020-2024)

The significant decline in total refinary production could be due to the global impact of COVID-19 pandamic which causes reduced petroleum consumption due to reduced transportation and travel restrictions.

The prolonged lower levels of fuel production might signal the beginning of a broader energy transition, as countries move toward cleaner, more sustainable sources of energy, impacting long-term demand for fossil fuels.
